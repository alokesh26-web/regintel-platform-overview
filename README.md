# RegIntel – Decision-Grade Regulatory Intelligence Platform

RegIntel is an AI-assisted regulatory intelligence platform designed to transform document-driven regulatory workflows into structured, explainable, and decision-ready systems.

## Purpose

The platform supports regulatory officers by converting multi-format submissions into structured datasets, validating them against regulatory rules, identifying compliance gaps, and generating risk scores with explainable reasoning.

## Key Capabilities

- Document AI and NLP-based extraction
- Regulatory data structuring
- Compliance validation and cross-document checks
- Risk scoring and anomaly detection
- Human-in-the-loop review
- Explainable outputs and audit trail
- Secure cloud-native architecture

## Proposed Use Case

The solution is proposed for CDSCO regulatory workflows covering drugs, medical devices, and clinical trial submissions.
